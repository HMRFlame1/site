HOW THE CHAMPIONSHIP WORKS

The GameCircuit Championship 2026 features 6 key events. Teams earn Series Points from Open Tournaments to qualify and seed for the Prestige League and future events.

1. KICKOFF TOURNAMENT
Free entry • 16-team Single Elimination
£50 prize pool • 100 Series Points for signing up & playing + placement bonuses
2. OPEN EVENT #1
£15 entry per team • 32-team Single Elimination
Bo3 until Winners Final & Losers Final • Prize pool based on sign-ups
3. PRESTIGE LEAGUE SEASON 1
16-team league for the top 16 teams by Series Points
£20 entry to lock in your spot (unpaid spots go to next in line)
4. OPEN EVENT #2
£15 entry per team • 32-team Single Elimination
Bo3 until Winners Final & Losers Final • Prize pool based on sign-ups
5. OPEN EVENT #3
£15 entry per team • 32-team Single Elimination
Bo3 until Winners Final & Losers Final • Prize pool based on sign-ups
6. CHAMPIONSHIP FINALS
16-team Pool Play + Double Elimination
Free entry for top 12 by Series Points • Final 4 spots via £20 qualifiers

Series Points determine seeding for leagues and future events.
