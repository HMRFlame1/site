HOW THE CHAMPIONSHIP WORKS

The GameCircuit Championship 2026 features 6 key events. Teams earn Series Points from Open Tournaments to qualify and seed for the Prestige League and future events.

1. KICKOFF TOURNAMENT
Free entry • 16-team Single Elimination • £50 prize pool • 100 Series Points
2. OPEN EVENT #1
£15 entry • 32-team Single Elimination • Bo3 • Prize pool based on sign-ups
3. PRESTIGE LEAGUE SEASON 1
16-team league for top 16 by Series Points • £20 entry to lock spot
4. OPEN EVENT #2
£15 entry • 32-team Single Elimination • Bo3 • Prize pool based on sign-ups
5. OPEN EVENT #3
£15 entry • 32-team Single Elimination • Bo3 • Prize pool based on sign-ups
6. CHAMPIONSHIP FINALS
16-team Pool Play + Double Elimination • Free for top 12 • £20 qualifiers for final spots

Series Points determine seeding for leagues and future events.
