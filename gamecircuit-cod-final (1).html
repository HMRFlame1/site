THE GAMECIRCUIT BLACK OPS 2 COMPETITIVE SEASON

The GameCircuit Black Ops 2 Competitive Season is a community-driven, open-format competitive series built to bring back the classic circuit experience from the golden era of esports. Designed for all skill levels, GameCircuit combines a Prestige League, monthly open tournaments, and a year-ending European Championship — giving every team a clear path to compete, improve, and earn their place among the best.

We're taking it back to the glory days! Enjoy no GA's, straightforward bans, and the timeless thrill of a classic title — console only.

Earn Series Points by placing well in tournaments and league matches. These points determine your seeding for the next event.

Across the year, teams compete in the GameCircuit season with multiple GC Open events. Every match and tournament matters. Points earned feed into seeding and qualification for the Championship. Whether you're a seasoned competitor or an up-and-coming roster, the circuit rewards consistency, grind, and passion.

  • Transparent Progression: Earn points, climb standings, and fight for qualification
  • Multiple Entry Points: Prestige League, open tournaments, and playoffs throughout the year
  • Casted Events & Player Features: Tournament coverage bringing attention to teams and players
  • Prize Payouts: Rewarding top placements at league playoffs, opens, and the Championship
  • Community-Focused: Competitive structure built for players, not corporations

You must have a GameCircuit account to be accepted into any event or tournament.
